# Aggressive Portfolio Management Playbook

## 1. Core Strategy
- **Strategy Type**: Momentum-Driven
- **Objective**: Maximize returns by capitalizing on short to medium-term price movements. Focus on stocks with strong upward momentum and clear catalysts.

## 2. Asset Selection
- **Stock Picking Criteria**: 
  - **Momentum Indicators**: Prioritize stocks with high relative strength index (RSI) and positive moving average convergence divergence (MACD).
  - **Catalysts**: Look for upcoming earnings reports, product launches, or industry news that could drive price action.
  - **Volume**: Ensure high trading volume to confirm momentum and facilitate liquidity.
- **Sector Focus**: 
  - **Technology**: Due to rapid innovation and frequent catalysts.
  - **Healthcare**: Driven by drug approvals and technological advancements.
  - **Consumer Discretionary**: Benefiting from economic cycles and consumer trends.
- **ETFs**: Utilize sector-specific ETFs to gain exposure to broader trends when individual stock opportunities are limited.

## 3. Trade Horizon
- **Holding Period**: 1 to 4 weeks
  - Positions will be held as long as momentum persists and catalysts remain relevant. Quick exits will be made if momentum fades or catalysts fail to materialize.

## 4. Risk Profile
- **Stop-Loss**: 5% to 8%
  - Tight stop-losses to cut losses quickly and protect capital.
- **Take-Profit**: 15% to 25%
  - Aggressive profit targets to capitalize on strong momentum moves.
- **Position Sizing**: 
  - Concentrated positions with 15% to 25% of capital per trade to maximize impact of high-conviction ideas.

## 5. Data Usage
- **Technical Analysis**: Primary tool for identifying momentum and entry/exit points.
- **Fundamentals**: Secondary consideration to ensure underlying financial health and support for momentum.
- **News and Catalysts**: Monitor FINRA news for potential market-moving events.
- **Macro Data**: Use FRED macro data to understand broader economic trends that could impact sector performance.

## Execution Plan
- **Daily Review**: Analyze market conditions and adjust positions based on momentum shifts and news developments.
- **Portfolio Monitoring**: Continuously monitor open positions for adherence to stop-loss and take-profit levels.
- **Benchmark**: Aim to outperform the S&P 500 Index by maintaining a high Information Ratio through disciplined execution of the momentum strategy.
