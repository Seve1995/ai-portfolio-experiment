```markdown
# BALANCED PORTFOLIO MANAGER - INVESTMENT PLAYBOOK

## 1. CORE STRATEGY: BALANCED MULTI-FACTOR APPROACH
**Philosophy:** Seek consistent returns through a balanced mix of growth, value, and momentum factors. Avoid extreme concentration in any single style. Prioritize companies with reasonable valuations, sustainable growth prospects, and positive price momentum.

**Implementation:**
- 40% allocation to quality growth stocks (strong earnings growth, reasonable valuations)
- 40% allocation to value opportunities (margin of safety, improving fundamentals)
- 20% allocation to momentum positions (strong relative strength, sector leadership)
- Always maintain sector diversification (no more than 25% in any single sector)

## 2. ASSET SELECTION CRITERIA

### Primary Filters:
- Market Cap: $2B+ for individual stocks (avoid micro-caps)
- Price: $10+ (additional buffer above $5 minimum)
- Liquidity: Minimum 500k average daily volume
- Financial Health: Positive earnings, manageable debt (debt/equity < 1.5 for most positions)

### Sector Preferences (Rotational Focus):
**Core Holdings (60% allocation):**
- Technology (cloud computing, semiconductors, software)
- Healthcare (pharma, medical devices, biotech with approved products)
- Financials (large banks, insurance with strong balance sheets)
- Consumer Staples (defensive positioning)

**Tactical Opportunities (40% allocation):**
- Industrials (infrastructure, aerospace)
- Energy (integrated majors, renewables)
- Consumer Discretionary (select retailers, automotive)

### ETF Usage:
- Deploy ETFs for sector exposure when individual stock selection is challenging
- Use broad market ETFs (SPY, QQQ) for cash deployment during uncertain periods
- Maximum 30% portfolio allocation to ETFs

## 3. TRADE HORIZON
**Primary Holding Period:** 2-6 months
- Growth positions: 3-6 months (allow thesis to develop)
- Value positions: 4-8 months (patience for revaluation)
- Momentum positions: 1-3 months (capture trend acceleration)

**Review Schedule:**
- Weekly: Monitor all positions for thesis deterioration
- Monthly: Full portfolio review and rebalancing consideration
- Quarterly: Strategic allocation adjustment based on macro environment

## 4. RISK MANAGEMENT PROTOCOL

### Position Sizing:
- Core positions: 10-15% of portfolio
- Tactical positions: 5-10% of portfolio
- Maximum 12 open positions (below 15 limit for flexibility)
- Maximum 25% sector exposure
- Maximum 35% single-style exposure (growth/value/momentum)

### Stop-Loss Framework:
**Primary Stop-Loss:** 15% from entry price
- Growth stocks: 15-18% (allow for volatility)
- Value stocks: 12-15% (tighter on value traps)
- Momentum stocks: 10-12% (quick exit on trend break)

**Trailing Stops:** Implement after 10% gain
- Initial: 10% trailing stop
- After 15% gain: 12% trailing stop
- After 25% gain: 15% trailing stop

### Take-Profit Levels:
**Scaled Profit Taking:**
- 20% gain: Trim 25% of position
- 30% gain: Trim another 25%
- 40% gain: Evaluate full exit or trail stop
- Always leave runners with trailing stops for exceptional performers

### Portfolio-Level Risk Controls:
- Maximum single position loss: 1.5% of total portfolio
- Maximum daily portfolio drawdown: 2% trigger for review
- Maximum portfolio drawdown: 15% hard limit (reduce risk exposure)

## 5. DATA USAGE PRIORITIZATION

### Daily Analysis (Highest Priority):
1. **Technical Analysis (40% weight):**
   - 50/200-day moving average relationships
   - Relative Strength vs. sector and SPY
   - Volume patterns on key price levels
   - Support/resistance identification

2. **Fundamental Screens (30% weight):**
   - Earnings surprises/revisions
   - Valuation metrics (P/E, P/S relative to history)
   - Institutional ownership trends
   - Short interest changes

3. **News & Sentiment (20% weight):**
   - FINRA news for corporate developments
   - Sector rotation signals
   - Analyst rating changes (focus on consensus shifts)

4. **Macro Indicators (10% weight):**
   - FRED data for economic health checks
   - Yield curve monitoring
   - VIX levels for market sentiment

### Weekly Deep Dives:
- Full fundamental review of 2-3 holdings
- Sector relative strength analysis
- Portfolio correlation check

### Monthly Macro Assessment:
- Economic cycle positioning
- Interest rate environment adaptation
- Sector weight adjustment based on macro outlook
```

*This playbook establishes a disciplined, repeatable process that balances growth potential with risk management, designed to outperform in various market conditions while maintaining controlled drawdowns.*
