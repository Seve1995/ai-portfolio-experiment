# Balanced Portfolio Manager Playbook

## 1. Core Strategy
- **Strategy Type**: Balanced approach combining value and growth investing.
- **Objective**: Achieve consistent returns with controlled drawdowns under 15% by balancing risk and reward.

## 2. Asset Selection
- **Stock Selection Criteria**:
  - **Value Stocks**: Focus on companies with strong fundamentals, low P/E ratios, and solid dividend yields.
  - **Growth Stocks**: Target companies with high revenue growth potential and strong market positioning.
  - **Momentum Stocks**: Occasionally incorporate stocks with strong recent price performance to capture short-term trends.
- **Sector Allocation**:
  - Diversify across sectors with a preference for Technology, Healthcare, Consumer Discretionary, and Financials.
  - Maintain flexibility to adjust sector weights based on macroeconomic conditions and sector performance.
- **ETFs**: Utilize sector and broad market ETFs to gain exposure to specific themes or hedge against market volatility.

## 3. Trade Horizon
- **Holding Period**: Typically 1 to 3 months, with flexibility to adjust based on market conditions and individual stock performance.

## 4. Risk Profile
- **Position Sizing**: Allocate 10% to 15% of capital per position, with a maximum of 15 open positions.
- **Stop-Loss**: Set stop-loss orders at 10% below the purchase price to limit downside risk.
- **Take-Profit**: Target a take-profit level of 20% to 30% above the purchase price to capture gains while allowing for upside potential.

## 5. Data Usage
- **Fundamentals**: Prioritize fundamental analysis to assess company financial health, valuation, and growth prospects.
- **Technicals**: Use technical indicators to identify entry and exit points, focusing on moving averages, RSI, and MACD.
- **Macro Data**: Monitor FRED macroeconomic indicators to understand broader economic trends and adjust sector allocations accordingly.
- **News and Sentiment**: Stay informed with FINRA news to gauge market sentiment and identify potential catalysts or risks.

## Summary
This playbook outlines a balanced investment strategy that seeks to optimize risk and reward through a diversified portfolio of value and growth stocks, with a disciplined approach to risk management and data-driven decision-making.
